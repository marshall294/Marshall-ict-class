Year 1 ICT – Week 1: Technology Around Us

Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Identify different examples of technology around us.
  • Explain how some technology works in simple terms.
  • Explain how technology helps us in daily life.
  • Locate and point out examples of technology in the classroom.

Success Criteria

  • ✔️ I can explain how some examples of technology work.
  • ✔️ I can explain how some technology helps us.
  • ✔️ I can find examples of technology in the classroom.

What is Technology?

Technology is anything created by people to help us solve problems or make life easier. It can be machines, tools, or systems.

Example: A computer helps us to type, draw, and connect to the internet.
Example: A fan helps us stay cool when the weather is hot.
Example: A school projector helps the teacher show lessons to the whole class.

Types of Technology Around Us

  • At Home: TV, fridge, microwave, washing machine.
  • At School: Computer, projector, smart board, printer.
  • Outside: Cars, traffic lights, street lights, ATM machines.

How Does Technology Help Us?

  • It saves time. (Example: Washing machine washes clothes faster than by hand.)
  • It makes communication easy. (Example: Phones help us talk to people far away.)
  • It makes learning fun. (Example: Computers and projectors show videos and pictures.)
  • It keeps us safe. (Example: Traffic lights help cars and people move safely.)

Practical Activity

  1. Look around your classroom. Can you see at least 3 examples of technology?
  2. Talk with your partner: How does one of these technologies help your class?
  3. Draw a picture of your favourite technology at home or school.

Assessment – Check Your Understanding

1. Which of these is a technology?

2. Why do we use a traffic light?

3. Point to something in your classroom that is technology.

(Ask the student to physically point.)
