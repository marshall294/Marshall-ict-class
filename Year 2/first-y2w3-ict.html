Information Technology (IT) means using computers and digital tools to collect, store, and share information. IT is not only in schools ‚Äî it is everywhere in our daily lives.

Sorting IT: IT can be grouped by places where it is found ‚Äî like school, home, hospital, bank, or shop.

Why IT is useful:

  • It makes work faster and easier.
  • It helps people share information quickly.
  • It keeps records safe and organised.

Real-life example: Without IT, a doctor would write all patient details on paper. With IT, all records are stored on a computer and can be found quickly.

Key Concepts

  • IT (Information Technology): Using digital tools like computers, tablets, and machines to handle information.
  • ATM: A machine at the bank that lets people take money or check balances.
  • Scanner: A device that reads barcodes or documents.
  • Sorting IT: Grouping IT devices by where they are used or what they are used for.
