Subject Knowledge

  • The internet is a global system of interconnected computer networks.
  • Networks are connected through cables, satellites, and wireless signals.
  • Data travels in small units called packets that move across networks.
  • Security measures like firewalls and passwords protect networks from harm.

Key Concepts and Skills

  • Understanding the internet as a physical and virtual structure.
  • Recognizing how networks share and exchange information.
  • Discussing the importance of protecting networks.
  • Collaborative group work and discussion.
