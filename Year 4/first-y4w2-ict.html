Year 4 Computing — Week 2

Topic: The Internet | Date: Week 2

Objective: Students will recognise how networked devices make up the internet.

Learning Objective

At the end of the lesson, students should be able to explain what networked devices are, how they connect, and how they work together to form the internet.

Success Criteria

  • ✔️ I can describe networked devices and how they connect.
  • ✔️ I can explain that the internet provides many different services.
  • ✔️ I can recognise that the World Wide Web contains websites and web pages.

Notes and Explanations

What are networked devices?
Networked devices are machines like computers, tablets, smartphones, printers, and servers that are connected together so they can share information. They connect through cables, Wi-Fi, or satellites.

How do they connect?
- A router or modem helps connect devices to the internet.
- Devices send and receive information in the form of data packets.
- These packets travel across networks all over the world.

What is the internet?
The internet is a network of networks. It connects millions of devices worldwide so people can share information, communicate, and access services.

Services of the internet:

  • Email – send and receive messages instantly.
  • World Wide Web (WWW) – a collection of websites and web pages.
  • Online video and music – streaming platforms like YouTube and Spotify.
  • Cloud storage – saving files online using Google Drive or Dropbox.
  • Social media – sharing posts and chatting with friends worldwide.

The World Wide Web (WWW):
The WWW is part of the internet. It is made up of websites and web pages linked together using hyperlinks. To visit a website, you type its address (URL) into a web browser.

Real-life example:
When you search for a song on YouTube:
- Your device (networked device) sends a request to YouTube’s server.
- The internet carries this request in data packets.
- The server responds by sending back the video data.
- The video appears on your screen through the World Wide Web.

Key Concepts

  • Networked devices are connected machines that share information.
  • The internet is a global network of networks.
  • The internet provides services like email, websites, and cloud storage.
  • The World Wide Web is made up of websites and web pages.
