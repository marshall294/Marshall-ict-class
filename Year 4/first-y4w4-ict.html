Key Concepts

  • Internet: A global network that connects computers all over the world.
  • World Wide Web (WWW): A collection of information accessed through the Internet.
  • Website: A place on the web where information like text, images, and videos can be found.
  • Media: Content such as pictures, videos, and text.
  • Online Content: Information that people can upload or share on the Internet.

Examples of Online Content

Example 1: Posting a photo on a class blog.
Example 2: Adding a video to a learning platform.
Example 3: Writing and sharing a story online.
Example 4: Accessing a school website to find homework instructions.
