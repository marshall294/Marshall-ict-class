Key Concepts

  • Search Engine: A program that searches the web for information (e.g., Google, Bing).
  • Keywords: Important words you type into a search box to find information quickly.
  • Refining a Search: Adjusting or narrowing down your search terms to get more accurate results.
  • Algorithm: The set of rules a search engine uses to decide which results to show first.
  • Sponsored Results: Ads that appear at the top of search results ‚Äî not always the best or most accurate information.
