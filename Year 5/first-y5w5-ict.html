Key Concepts

  • Ranking: The order in which search results are shown.
  • Relevance: How closely the search result matches what was searched for.
  • Trustworthiness: Websites from reliable sources are ranked higher.
  • Popularity: Pages that are visited often may be ranked higher.
  • Algorithm: A set of rules that search engines follow to rank results.
