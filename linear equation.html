1. Understanding Linear Equations

A linear equation is an equation where the highest power of the variable is 1. In IGCSE, the variable is usually x. The aim is to find the value of x that makes the equation true.

  • The equation has an equals sign (=).
  • Both sides must always stay equal.
  • Whatever you do to one side, you must do to the other.
\(x + 5 = 12\)
This equation is balanced. Our job is to isolate x.
